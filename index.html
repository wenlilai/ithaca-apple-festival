We Welcome You!

The Downtown Ithaca is pleased to announce the 39th Annual Apple Harvest Festival that will be held this fall and presented by Tompkins Trust Company.

Upon the upcoming three days, we will be celebrating many wonderful cider makers, cider donuts, and apple growers as a token of appreciation. In this festival, we will be hosting fresh apples, seasonal baked goods, and a variety of food trucks offering tasty bites. Not to mention, we will also be hosting live music and entertainment to enlight the vibes while enjoying the refreshing cider and juicy apples. There will also be apple-inspired products for sale!

The Downtown Ithaca of Apple Harvest Festival welcomes all of you to join our festival!


WHEN?

Friday, Oct 1: 12pm - 6pm
Saturday, Oct 2: 10am - 6pm
Sunday, Oct 3: 10am - 6pm

WHERE?

ABOUT

The 2021 Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival is wonderful and exciting festival that hosts fresh baked goods, fresh apples, entertainment, and live performances to bring the whole community together. Visitors and residents can witness and enjoy the wonderful entertainment while enjoying the fresh baked goods and apple sold there! Furthermore, all visitors is also able to experience and enjoy the outdoor pedestrian shopping mall, The Ithaca Commons. There are plenty of wonderful shops there.

In this festival, there are various vendors selling plenty of apples, cider donuts, and baked goods. Not to mention, there are plenty of various beverages sold there and of course, the main product will be apple and more apple juices. It is indeed a wonderful experience and you will indeed have a good time here!
