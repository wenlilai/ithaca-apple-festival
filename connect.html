Apply for a spot at our festival

Our Apple Festival features various food vendors, artists, and crafters all over the state! Therefore, our vacancy of applying for a vendor is based on the first come first serve basis. The deadline for entries is on the Wednesday, September 1 2021. Applicants will receive an email confirmation at the start of the second week of September. Applications that are received after the deadline will be placed on the waiting list.
